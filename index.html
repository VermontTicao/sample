Noli Me Tangere Characters

Maria Clara		She is a very religious and a self effacing person. Maria Clara is the wife of Crisostomo Ibarra. A lot of people admire her.

Padre Damaso		He is a very conceited person, or in Tagalog, "Mayabang". A very toxic person that thinks he's superior to the people in the lower class. Padre Damaso is the father of the wealthy Crisostomo Ibarra.

Kapitan Tiago		The husband of Doña Pia Alba. He is also wealthy person. He is a powerful person, however he would abuse his powers and ill-treat his fellow people. Kapitan Tiago owns a lot of lands in the countryside.
